Sprint 1: My Identity and Values

Core personal values:

My core personal values are integrity, kindness and generosity. I like the saying “what goes around comes around”.

Strengths/Limitations:

Evaluate your strengths and limitations in terms of your learning and career development

I tend to work things out on my own ways. Most of the times this is good as when it comes to learning a new concept, working things out on my own helps me to fully understand the concept and to put it into practice. However, this also limits me with time management. As I may get stuck on a idea or concept and spend unnecessary amount of time. Asking for help and getting help from others can help fix/understand things more appropriately.

Identify which of your strengths might help you in your learning journey and how they intersect with learning obstacles.

My strengths: Strong work ethic and I finish work on time.

My strong work ethic has helped me get through a lot of challenges in life. As I have no prior background knowledge in programming, getting through Dev Academy will be another challenge that I will need to break through. This is where my strengths will come in handy.
