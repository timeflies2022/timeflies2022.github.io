Core personal values:

My core personal values are integrity, kindness and generosity. I like the saying “what goes around comes around”.

Everyday we have to fight ourselves to make ethical decisions. One moment when I made an ethical decision is back when I was much younger. While I was walking to the dairy there I spotted a $50 note drop from someones pocket and the person had no idea this had happened. I could have just picked up the money but I could not do that. I picked up the money and walked up to the person to return it to the owner. This was when I was 13 years old and $50 was a lot to me then. To this day I'm proud that I have made the right decision.
