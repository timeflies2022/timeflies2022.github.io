Sprint 5: Reflection

What are new things you have learnt about yourself and your ego due to the core learning?

Going over Core learning materials I learned how inefficiently I dealt with my stress. Previously when I had time to spare where I was not so pressured in running out of time, I dealt with stress just fine. However, with time being scarce I was mentally beading myself but thankfully by going over the materials each week. I came to a realization of this and how I need to change my way of releasing stress.

I’ve always considered myself as a quick learner. So, my egos and my confidence were high before foundation. In the last 5 weeks I learned that this in fact was not true at all.

What has surprised you the most about the core learning?

The importance of being honest about yourself and really paying attention to your inner thoughts. So you can evaluate yourself honestly and see what you can improve on.

What were the most challenging aspects of the core learning?

To be honest, all of the Core activities were challenging. Because they are not something I had encountered before. Starting from evaluating my values to listening and looping. At first they came to me as a challenge but I’m starting to get the hang of it.

Why do you think we, a programming school, are spending so much time focusing on core learning in a web development Bootcamp course?

I have been told that a software developer’s job is not all about coding. Your job will need you to be a member of a group and complete given tasks in a group together. Therefore, a lot of times you will in a meeting, sharing ideas and coming up with a solution with your team. In order to become a good member of a team you will need to understand your strengths and weaknesses to become an essential member.

Does the time you spent studying core learning here feel like a waste of time? Should you have just used that time to practise programming instead? Justify your answer.

The last 5 weeks, I have been given the opportunity to study myself and to really understand who I am. This was achieved through core study materials and I’m very thankful for it. We are living in a such busy world where we tend to lose our characteristics and become someone foreign. The core materials made sure and helped me to understood who I really am.
